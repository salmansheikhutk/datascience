Overview

Dataset: 569 samples with 30 features measuring tumor characteristics (radius, texture, perimeter, area, smoothness, etc.)

Goal: Classify tumors as benign or malignant

Method: Logistic regression with 80/20 train-test split

Outcome: High accuracy model with excellent precision and good recall
